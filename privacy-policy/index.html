Privacy Policy

The Aurora Chronicles — by Entropy

Effective date: February 14, 2026
Last updated: March 4, 2026

1. Who We Are

The Aurora Chronicles is developed by kittikun and published by Entropy, a sole proprietorship (個人事業主) based in Tokyo, Japan. This policy applies to The Aurora Chronicles on all platforms, including Steam, iOS, and Android.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the game experience:

  • Entropy ID (optional): If you create an Entropy ID for cross-platform saves, we collect your email address and authentication credentials via Firebase Authentication (Google).
  • Save Data: If you create an Entropy ID, your game progress is stored on Cloud Firestore (Google Firebase) and linked to your account for cross-platform access. On Steam, save data is also synced via Steam Cloud independently of your Entropy ID.
  • Choice Statistics: When you make in-game choices, your selections are recorded to show community statistics (e.g., what percentage of players chose each option). By default, choices are linked to an anonymous identifier automatically generated by Firebase on your device. If you later create an Entropy ID, this same identifier is retained — no new ID is created. This data is stored on Supabase (cloud database). Other players only see aggregated percentages (e.g., "65% of players chose option A"), never individual choices.
  • Analytics & Crash Reports: We collect anonymous gameplay analytics to improve game quality, identify bugs, and understand how players engage with content. This data may include session duration, device information (OS, language, screen resolution), app version, and in-game events such as episode progress and menu interactions. Analytics data is processed by Unity Technologies (Unity Gaming Services) and Google (Google Analytics for Firebase). On mobile, Firebase Crashlytics also collects crash reports including device type, OS version, and crash logs. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics, and this data cannot be linked to individual users.

If you play without creating an Entropy ID, your save data remains local to your device (or synced via Steam Cloud on Steam). Choice statistics are still recorded using your anonymous Firebase identifier, but this cannot be used to identify you personally.

3. What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not store or use IP addresses or location data for tracking purposes.
  • Analytics across all platforms are limited to anonymous, aggregated data (see Section 2). We do not use analytics to identify or track individual players.
  • We do not serve advertisements.
  • We do not sell, share, or transfer any personal data to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

The following third-party services may process data on our behalf:

  • Firebase (Google): Handles Entropy ID sign-in (Firebase Authentication) and stores cross-platform cloud saves (Cloud Firestore). Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase: Stores choice statistics data linked to your anonymous Firebase identifier (or Entropy ID, if created). Subject to Supabase's Privacy Policy.
  • Unity Gaming Services (Unity Technologies): Collects anonymous gameplay analytics across all platforms. Subject to Unity's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Analytics for Firebase & Firebase Crashlytics: Collects anonymous usage data across all platforms and crash data on mobile to help us improve the game. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Steam (Valve): Handles platform accounts, purchases, and Steam Cloud save sync. Subject to Steam's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple: Handles App Store purchases and accounts on iOS. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play: Handles purchases and accounts on Android. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

We do not have access to your payment information on any platform.

5. Analytics Data Retention

Anonymous analytics data is retained according to each provider's standard retention policies: Unity Gaming Services retains data for up to 12 months, and Google Analytics retains data for up to 14 months. These periods may be adjusted in their respective dashboards. Analytics data is anonymous and cannot be linked to individual users.

6. Data Storage & Security

Authentication data and cross-platform cloud saves are stored using Firebase (Google Cloud) infrastructure. Choice statistics are stored on Supabase. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

The Aurora Chronicles is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Your Rights

You may at any time:

  • Request a copy of your personal data
  • Request deletion of your Entropy ID and all associated data
  • Play the game without creating an account (local saves only)

To make a request, contact us using the details in Section 10 below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the game after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
